The inquiry flow

Start a conversation

Six questions, one per screen. Calm and unhurried, so it reads as the beginning of a real conversation rather than a form. It gently qualifies without gating: the budget question is required, because Brian cannot have an honest conversation without it, but every band is phrased with respect and “I would welcome guidance on this” is a legitimate answer.

It works with the keyboard, it survives with JavaScript switched off, and it ends on a confirmation that reinforces the promise: we will be in touch personally, within a couple of days. All three homepages link to it.

What to look for — and what I already know is wrong

  • All three share the same copy and the same hero photo on purpose. Only the structure differs, so the comparison is about ordering and emphasis, not about writing.
  • The photography is placeholder and is doing a lot of unearned work. None of these are pictures of Total Build homes. Gabe’s real shoot — Schwartz first — will change how all three read, and it will help A most, because A bets everything on the images.
  • There is no photograph of Brian. Every prototype shows a proportioned placeholder block where his portrait goes. This penalises C hardest, since C leads with him. Judge C on the layout, not on the grey rectangle.
  • The Schwartz residence has no photo, so where it appears it is shown honestly as an empty frame marked photography-pending.
  • The 1910 timeline is gone from all three homepages. One sentence survives inside “why work with us,” with a “Read our story” link. The full record moves to About, which does not exist yet — that link goes nowhere on purpose.
  • Direction B lost its thesis. B was built with the lineage as its structural spine. With the lineage demoted, its blueprint/ledger treatment is now a beautiful skin rather than a structural argument. Worth knowing before you pick it.
  • Mobile nav is a non-functional label in all three. Deliberate — these are design prototypes, not builds.
  • No certifications appear anywhere. None are confirmed, so none are printed. Same for years-in-business.
  • Phone shown is Joe’s confirmed (703) 634-9211. The live site says 703-489-9199. Still unresolved, still an SEO liability.
