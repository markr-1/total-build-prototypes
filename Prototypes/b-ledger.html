The name on the company is the name on the job.

Brian Smith studied communications at George Mason, then spent years in insurance restoration — until the industry’s assembly-line approach, transactional and often thankless, sent him back to the work his family had always done.

He has personally estimated and managed upwards of two thousand construction and remodeling projects. On a Total Build home, he is who you sit across from — not a sales desk, not a project manager. Brian.

Brian is the fourth generation of Smith builders; his great-grandfather began contracting in 1910.
