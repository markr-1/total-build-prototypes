The owner

You work directly with Brian.

We only take on a few homes a year — so the person who estimates your home is the person who builds it.

Brian Smith is the founder, owner, and General Contractor. He started the company in 2008 after years in insurance restoration, where the assembly-line approach felt transactional and often thankless. He has personally estimated and managed upwards of two thousand construction and remodeling projects.

Quality

Built level, square, and to last. The work behind the drywall is as sound as the work you can see.

Communication

You always know where your home stands. No long silences, no surprises on the invoice.

Trust

What we say holds. We show up when we said we would and finish what we started.

People

We build for the family who will live here, not for a portfolio. The relationship outlasts the punch list.

Brian is the fourth generation of Smith builders; his great-grandfather began contracting in 1910.
